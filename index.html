Rocket League Tribute

Overview

Rocket League is a fast paced, physics based "soccer with cars" video game developed by Psyonix and powered by Epic Games. Players use rocket-powered vehicles to hit a giant ball into the opposing team's goal in a 5-minute match, emphasizing teamwork, aerial maneuvers, and vehicle customization. The game is free-to-play, supports cross-platform play, and is popular in 1v1, 2v2, and 3v3 gamemodes.
